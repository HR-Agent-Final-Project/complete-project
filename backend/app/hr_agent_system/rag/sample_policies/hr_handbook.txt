COMPANY HR HANDBOOK — Sri Lanka Operations
==========================================

COMPANY VALUES
──────────────
Integrity:     We act with honesty and transparency in all interactions.
Innovation:    We embrace new ideas and continuously improve our processes.
Teamwork:      We collaborate and support each other to achieve shared goals.
Excellence:    We hold ourselves to the highest professional standards.
Respect:       We treat every individual with dignity and fairness.

CODE OF CONDUCT
───────────────
1. Professional Behaviour
   - Maintain respectful communication with all colleagues and clients
   - Avoid any form of discrimination, harassment, or bullying
   - Protect confidential company and client information
   - Use company resources responsibly and for business purposes only

2. Dress Code
   - Business casual Monday to Thursday
   - Smart casual on Fridays
   - Client meeting days: formal business attire

3. Digital Conduct
   - Company email and systems for business use only
   - Social media posts must not disclose confidential information
   - Mobile phone use restricted during core work hours

DISCIPLINARY PROCESS
────────────────────
Level 1 — Verbal Warning
  Issued for: minor policy violations, first-time attendance issues
  Recorded:   in employee HR file

Level 2 — Written Warning
  Issued for: repeated Level 1 offences, serious single violations
  Signed by:  employee and HR Manager

Level 3 — Final Warning
  Issued for: repeated Level 2 offences, gross misconduct
  Review:     HR Director involved

Level 4 — Termination
  Grounds:    Repeated final warnings, gross misconduct, fraud, violence,
              abandonment of work (3+ days absent without notice)
  Process:    HR Director approval, legal consultation recommended

PROBATION POLICY
────────────────
Duration:     6 months from start date
Review:       Formal review at 3 months and 6 months
Extensions:   Probation may be extended by up to 3 months with justification
Leave During Probation: Only Sick Leave and Casual Leave available
Confirmation: Written confirmation letter issued upon successful completion

RESIGNATION & NOTICE PERIOD
────────────────────────────
Notice Required:     1 month for all levels (unless contract specifies otherwise)
Garden Leave:        Company may place employee on garden leave during notice
Final Settlement:    Within 30 days of last working day
Exit Interview:      Mandatory with HR Manager
Certificate:         Service certificate issued within 14 days of last day

HEALTH & SAFETY
───────────────
First Aid:    First aid kits located at each floor reception
Emergency:    Fire exits marked; evacuation drill conducted quarterly
Accidents:    Report to HR immediately; incident form must be completed within 24 hours
Mental Health: Confidential counselling support available through HR

RECRUITMENT PROCESS
───────────────────
Step 1: Job requisition approved by Department Head and HR
Step 2: Job posted internally (3 days) then externally
Step 3: CV screening by HR and Department Head
Step 4: Technical interview (1-2 rounds)
Step 5: HR interview (culture fit, compensation discussion)
Step 6: Reference checks (2 professional references required)
Step 7: Offer letter issued and signed
Step 8: Background verification before start date

PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
──────────────────────
Review Cycle:   Quarterly review + Annual appraisal
Self-Assessment: Employee completes self-review form before review
Rating Scale:   Excellent (85-100) | Good (70-84) | Average (55-69) | Needs Improvement (<55)
Increment:      Annual salary increment based on annual appraisal rating
Promotion:      Eligibility after 1 year minimum in current role
